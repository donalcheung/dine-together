# UniTaskr Marketing Strategy for TableMesh

**Author:** Manus AI
**Date:** March 7, 2026

## 1. Overview

UniTaskr is a gig platform connecting businesses with over 350,000 students in the UK and USA, making it an ideal channel for growing TableMesh. The platform allows for hiring students for a variety of small-to-medium tasks, from content creation to on-the-ground promotion. Its core user base directly aligns with TableMesh's target demographic of young, social, and tech-savvy individuals.

The strategy is to leverage UniTaskr's **Free tier**, which allows for 5 tasks and 50 student hires per month, to achieve three primary goals:

1.  **Generate Authentic Content:** Source user-generated content (UGC) from real students for TikTok and YouTube.
2.  **Drive Early Adoption:** Encourage app downloads, reviews, and real-world usage through targeted tasks.
3.  **Gather Market Intelligence:** Collect feedback and data to inform product development and future marketing efforts.

## 3. UniTaskr Task Descriptions (Ready to Post)

Below are the detailed task descriptions, ready to be copied and pasted into UniTaskr.

### **Task 1: UGC TikTok Content**

*   **Task Title:** Create 3 Authentic TikTok Videos About Our New App, TableMesh!
*   **Category:** Social Media / Content Creation
*   **Budget:** $85
*   **Description:**

> We are looking for 3 creative TikTok users to produce one authentic, engaging video each about our new social dining app, TableMesh. TableMesh helps people find others to dine with, whether you're new to a city, your friends are busy, or you just want to meet new people.
>
> **Video Brief (Choose one):**
> 1.  **Problem/Solution:** A skit showing the frustration of trying to organize group dinners in a chaotic group chat, ending with the discovery of TableMesh as the easy solution.
> 2.  **POV (Point of View):** "POV: You just moved to a new city and don't want to eat alone every night." Show yourself finding a cool dinner event on TableMesh.
> 3.  **Storytime:** "How I met my new best friends at a random dinner." Tell a quick, fun story about using the app to join a group meal.
>
> **Requirements:**
> - Video must be 15-45 seconds.
> - Mention "TableMesh" clearly (verbally or with on-screen text).
> - Use trending audio if it fits the video concept.
> - Be authentic! We want content that feels real, not like a corporate ad.
> - You will deliver the final video file to us (for our own social channels) AND post it on your personal TikTok account, tagging our account (if we have one) and using #TableMesh.
>
> **To Apply:** Please send a link to your TikTok profile.

### **Task 2: Campus Brand Ambassador**

*   **Task Title:** Campus Ambassador for New Social Dining App (Your University)
*   **Category:** Brand Ambassador / Promo Staff
*   **Budget:** $75
*   **Description:**

> We're looking for an outgoing, social student to be the first TableMesh ambassador at your university!
>
> TableMesh is a new app that helps people connect over food. Your mission, should you choose to accept it, is to get the word out on campus.
>
> **Your Tasks:**
> 1.  **Tell Your Friends:** Genuinely introduce the app to at least 10-15 friends and people in your social circles who you think would love it.
> 2.  **Host One Dinner:** Use the app to create and host one group dinner at a popular, affordable restaurant near campus. We will cover up to $25 of your personal meal cost for this dinner (on top of your task payment).
> 3.  **Post a Story:** Post at least one Instagram or TikTok story during your hosted dinner, showing your group and tagging the app.
> 4.  **Feedback:** Provide a short summary of people's reactions and any feedback you received.
>
> **Requirements:**
> - Must be a current student at the specified university.
> - Active in campus life (clubs, sports, social groups) is a huge plus.
> - Must have a public Instagram or TikTok profile.
>
> **To Apply:** Tell us your university and briefly describe why you'd be a great ambassador for a social dining app.

### **Task 3: Product Testing & App Review**

*   **Task Title:** Test Our New iOS App, TableMesh, and Leave an Honest App Store Review
*   **Category:** Product Testing / Market Research
*   **Budget:** $10
*   **Description:**

> We've just launched our new social dining app, TableMesh, on the iOS App Store and need your help!
>
> We are looking for 5 students to download the app, test its core features, and leave an honest, thoughtful review on the App Store based on your experience.
>
> **Your Tasks:**
> 1.  Download TableMesh from the App Store (we will provide the link).
> 2.  Create a profile.
> 3.  Create one "Dining Request" (you can cancel it after).
> 4.  Browse and view at least 3 other dining requests.
> 5.  Leave an honest review on the App Store (2-3 sentences).
> 6.  Send us a screenshot of your review and 1-2 sentences of any private feedback or bugs you found.
>
> **Requirements:**
> - Must have an iPhone.
> - Your feedback is incredibly valuable, so please be thoughtful!
